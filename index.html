About

I'm a third-year part-time Electrical and Electronics Engineering student at the University of Western Australia, based in Perth, WA.

I love the full stack of hardware — from schematic design and PCB layout in KiCad, to 3D-printed enclosures in Fusion 360, to embedded firmware running on ESP32 and Arduino platforms.

My projects span wireless communication, motor control, retro gaming consoles, and custom development boards — all designed and built from scratch.
